Outfit recommendation based on style

Most of current approaches of outfit generation for recommendation focus on style of pair-wise fashion items in an outfit rather than a global style of all outfits. Focusing on global styles of outfits offers more effective and accurate information for outfit recommendation.

We propose to explore adversarial auto encoder to extract the global styles from outfits, an unsupervised method to fast learn the global information of outfits.

Label Refinery and its applications
